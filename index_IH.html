Configuarations The list of configurations contains all configuarations that have been published.
Download jobs The first step is to download the feeds, downloads are possible for configurations with status "ready for download". If the last download is from today, the download will be skipped and the config status will be changed to "ready to import" tight away.
Import jobs After successful download, configurations with status "ready for import" can be imported .
Reset jobs If a job hangs, runs through, is failed or successful, you can reset it by clicking .
